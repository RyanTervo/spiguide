Things to Do in South Padre Island

Explore adventures, entertainment, and hidden gems along the Gulf Coast.

Outdoor Adventures

Parasailing

Soar above the island and take in stunning views from the sky.

Beach Horseback Riding

Enjoy a relaxing ride along the shoreline at sunset.

Sandcastle Lessons

Learn from local pros how to build stunning sand sculptures.

Water Activities

Dolphin Tours

Hop aboard and spot dolphins in their natural habitat.

Deep Sea Fishing

Fish for red snapper, kingfish, and more in the Gulf waters.

Kayaking & Paddleboarding

Glide across the Laguna Madre for a peaceful water experience.

Family-Friendly Activities

Sea Turtle Inc.

Visit the turtle rescue center and learn about marine conservation.

Beach Park at Isla Blanca

A family water park with slides, lazy river, and beachfront fun.

Mini Golf & Go-Karts

Classic family fun at Island Fun Park and Gravity Park.
